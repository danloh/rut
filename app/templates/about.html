About


Readup.Tips' mission is to share and grow the knowledge about how to grow one's knowledge. The mission based on the assumptions as follows:
1. The best way to grow one's knowledge is reading, reading books, documentary, atlas, online course, etc., more accurate and systematic;
2. Bias, standpoint and perspective of the author, is inevitable and reasonable for each book, to read up on a subject, we should go through a list of books by different authors;
3. Reading is costly, time or money, and there are huge number of books on each specific subject, selecting a right list is important.

Let the books (or documentary, online-course) and the readers find each other with the help from who have read them.
We believe that the experience or roadmap about how you learned something is also a huge treasure: which book or course or a list of books is better? you should have many ideas, So post to share them, the rest of the world can benefit from your sharing, you can also benefit from other's sharing.
You can also consider it as a tool to collect or share your reading list/ to-read list/ have-read list.

We are constantly improving the Service, may add or remove functionalities or features. We appreciate any suggestion, please contact us via readup.tips@gmail.com

Signing Up

You can customize your profile so that other people can get a better sense of who you are. You can choose a profile picture that represents you, change a nickname which is different from the default name ie. your SNS username, add a bio to yuor profile. Such profile information is public.

Writing on Readup.Tips

You can write, comment, post review and book list on Readup.Tips with Markdown, which is an easy-to-read, easy-to-write syntax for formatting plain text.
The details information of items, description of tags can be provided and modified by any logged-in user.

Post
Post is an item list(like book list) on a specific subject, we call it read-up list. You can add any item like book, online course, documentary etc. to it and add some reading tips or recommendation for each item.
Some approved contributpr or even everyone can edit a post if the creator set it be.

Clips
Clip is your quote excerpt from a book or other items you read, or a spark of insprition come from your reading, You can post it to track your reading-up process.

Review
You can post a review on a book or an online course.

Challenge
If you find some read-up list useful, you can star it, thus you create a bookmark for easier access, appreciate the creators' work and show your taste. You can take a read-up list(post) as challenge of the year or month or week or even of a day. there are some services to help you track your challenge:
You can set a deadline, and excerpt clips track your reading, and review it when you get an item done.

Request
If you did not find anything interesting, you can send a request on any subject, like 'I want to learn Python, which books or course is good?', then share and invite some experts to answer.
